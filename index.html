About Us:

AVA Maternity and Neuro-Psychiatry Clinic is a multi-specialty healthcare center committed to the complete well-being of both men and women. We offer expert medical care in maternity, infertility, sexual health, and mental wellness — all under one roof.

At AVA Clinic, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate healthcare for individuals of all genders, addressing both physical and mental health needs. Our integrated approach brings together specialized services to support every patient’s overall well-being — body and mind.

Our team includes Dr. Amrita Sandhu (MBBS, MS – Obstetrics & Gynaecology), a specialist in women’s health, maternity care, infertility treatment, and intimate wellness. She provides personalized care to support women at every stage — from reproductive health to postnatal recovery.

Alongside, Dr. Vasu Kalkat (MBBS, MD – Psychiatry) leads our mental health and male wellness services. With a focus on brain and mind disorders, male infertility, and sexual dysfunction, he ensures compassionate and confidential support for men’s psychological and reproductive health.
